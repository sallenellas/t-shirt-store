Before You Begin...
Step 1: All shirts are $20 by default, regardless of size
Step 2: Check the box for size, select a color, and enter a quantity
Step 3: Decide on a material
Step 4: Check the Price Table to see the cost per shirt
Step 5: Click 'Add To Cart' to see what you have ordered on the right (you can add several shirts of varying colors and quantities at once)
Step 6: Click 'View Cart' button to keep track of what you have ordered
Step 7: Enter your shipping information
Step 8: Verify that all of your shipping and order details are correct before submitting
Step 9: That's It!
